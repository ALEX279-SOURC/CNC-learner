Measuring instruments

Vernier caliper

👉List count of simple vernier caliper = 0.02mm

👉List count of digital vernier caliper = 0.01mm

            

             
👉how to calculate list count
 
                
List count = main scale - vernier scale
= 1 - 49(main scale)/ 50(vernier scale)

                
= 1 - 0.98

                
= 0.02 mm

Outside Micrometre

👉List count of simple Micrometre = 0.01mm

👉List count of digital Micrometre = 0.001mm ( 10 micron)

            

             
👉how to calculate list count
 
                
List count = main scale × 0.5 ÷ 50 
= 1 × 0.5 ÷ 50 

                
= 0.01 mm
